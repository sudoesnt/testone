Detailed Home Information

Construction & Materials

Braeridge House features a modern design with high-quality construction materials. The exterior combines fiber cement siding with architectural stone accents and large energy-efficient windows. The roof is covered with architectural shingles rated for 30 years with seamless gutters throughout.

Interior walls are constructed with 2x6 framing for better insulation and soundproofing between rooms. Interior doors are solid core for improved sound isolation. Flooring throughout the main living areas is engineered hardwood with tile in bathrooms and laundry areas.

Neighborhood & Location

Braeridge House is located in the desirable Peaceful Hills neighborhood, known for its tree-lined streets and community parks. The property sits at the end of a quiet cul-de-sac, providing extra privacy and minimal through traffic.

The home is positioned to take advantage of southern exposure in the backyard, maximizing natural light. The neighborhood features sidewalks throughout and is within walking distance (0.5 miles) to Tranquil Elementary School and Community Park.

Recent Improvements & Maintenance

Since construction in 2020, the following improvements have been made:

  • 2022: Installation of custom built-in cabinetry in home office
  • 2023: Expansion of back patio with pavers and built-in fire pit
  • 2024: Upgrade of smart home system to include voice control throughout
  • 2025: Addition of professional landscaping with native, drought-resistant plants

Regular maintenance includes annual HVAC servicing, quarterly pest control treatments, and bi-annual gutter cleaning.

About Our Home

A Story of Design and Comfort

Built in 2020, Braeridge House is a 3,200 square foot modern residence that perfectly balances sophisticated design with everyday functionality.
