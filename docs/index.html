Detailed Home Information

Construction & History

Braeridge House features a unique Nantucket-style design with a fascinating history. Originally a small shanty, the structure was lifted and significantly expanded to create the current 1,500 square foot residence. The property stands out with its two massive concrete block retaining walls that support the elevated structure.

The home has a distinctive layout with no traditional front door. Instead, it features two side doors that open onto charming decks on either side of the house, offering different vantage points to enjoy the surrounding natural beauty.

Location & Views

Braeridge House is perched at an impressive 874 feet elevation on a scenic hill, providing breathtaking views of Thompson Pond. This elevated position offers privacy and an exceptional natural setting.

One of the property's unique advantages is pond access through the neighbor's yard, allowing residents to enjoy water activities with convenient access to Thompson Pond.

Recent Improvements & Maintenance

Since construction in 2009, the following improvements have been made:

  • 2010: Installation of custom built-in cabinetry in home office
  • 2011: Expansion of back patio with pavers and built-in fire pit
  • 2012: Upgrade of smart home system to include voice control throughout
  • 2013: Addition of professional landscaping with native, drought-resistant plants

Regular maintenance includes annual HVAC servicing, quarterly pest control treatments, and bi-annual gutter cleaning.

About Our Home

A Story of Transformation and Character

Built in 2009, Braeridge House is a 1,500 square foot Nantucket-style residence with a unique history. Originally a modest shanty, the structure was lifted and expanded to create a charming home with distinctive character.

Perched at 874 feet elevation on a hill overlooking Thompson Pond, our home offers stunning views and privacy while allowing pond access through the neighbor's yard.

The unique design features no traditional front door, but rather two side doors opening onto decks, creating a seamless flow between indoor and outdoor living spaces.

With state-of-the-art security features including an advanced security system and integrated hardwired cameras with streaming capability, Braeridge House represents comfortable living with modern conveniences.
